Vertebra Mouse

An analysis was conducted in ANSYS to determine the impact force required to fracture the spinal cord in a mouse's vertebral column. This study considered all relevant physical and biological parameters, allowing for precise results regarding the threshold of resistance in the vertebral structure.

IK Kinova

In a practical session, the inverse kinematics for a Kinova robotic arm were successfully calculated. This involved determining the precise joint angles needed to position the end effector at specified coordinates. The task required solving nonlinear equations while considering the robot’s degrees of freedom and joint limitations. The implementation was carried out smoothly, resulting in accurate control of the robotic arm's movements.
