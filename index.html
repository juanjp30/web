My Portfolio

Design

Rover Resilience

Resilience was an interdisciplinary academic team dedicated to creating an autonomous rover with advanced detection and energy management systems for planetary exploration missions. The project focused on developing an adaptable vehicle with modular interfaces and the ability to incorporate technological upgrades, ensuring long-term performance.

Mouse Vertebra

One analysis was conducted in ANSYS to determine the impact force required to fracture the spinal cord in a mouse's vertebral column. This study considered all relevant physical and biological parameters, allowing for precise results regarding the threshold of resistance in the vertebral structure.

Virtual Reality

Anaglyph Filtering

This project focused on the analysis of 3D images, where techniques such as wavelength filtering (anaglyph) and spatial displacement were applied, including the side-by-side method for parallel and cross vision, as well as the top-down format. These techniques enabled the creation and visualization of three-dimensional images, providing an immersive experience in visual analysis.

Rice Factory

This proposal was developed with the aim of providing an immersive virtual environment of a rice production plant using AFrame. With this tool, the goal is to offer an interactive and realistic representation of the production process, facilitating the understanding and visualization of each stage within a three-dimensional environment.

Data Science

Database

This project consisted of creating a database for a fictional company using MySQL, with the goal of managing employees and departments. Throughout the project, essential operations were implemented, such as table creation, data insertion, basic and advanced queries, and updating information, like increasing salaries in a specific department. Techniques for exporting and importing data to and from Excel files were also addressed, demonstrating the versatility and power of MySQL for managing business databases.

Contact List

This project involved the creation of a contact agenda using a simple database that included basic information such as name, phone number, and email. The contacts were managed in a tabular format, facilitating the organization and access to personal information through tools like Excel and Python programming. The goal was to optimize the storage and manipulation of personal data to improve efficiency in querying and managing contact information.

Automation

Air Flow Control

This project focused on the forward and backward control of multiple pneumatic cylinders, simulated in FluidSIM software and controlled by a PLC, in order to follow a predefined sequence. The goal was to optimize the operation of the cylinders in a controlled environment, improving the precision and efficiency of automated processes.

Trophy Factory

For this project, the simulation of the actuators and sensors in a trophy manufacturing plant was proposed, controlled by a PLC programmed in TIA Portal using subroutines and simulated in FluidSIM. This simulation allowed for an accurate representation of the automation of the production process, optimizing the control and monitoring of the various devices involved.
